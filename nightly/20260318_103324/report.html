Overall Assessment

However, coverage is highly uneven — Conv & Pooling, SSM, and most of Reduce/Quantize/Sampling are effectively unstarted, and several implemented ops have critical gaps in either test coverage or benchmark harness health. The most urgent correctness issues are the rope/dropout test failures in Elementwise, the gated_deltanet_chunkwise performance collapse (0.12x), and the three failing Reduce norm ops.
